Mahatma Gandhi

The man Who Fought for Indian independence (1915–1947)

Early life and background

  • Full-name - Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi
  • 1869 - born on 2 October 1869 into a Gujarati family in Porbandar Gujarat!
  • 1988 - He studied law in England from 1888 to 1891!

    • Civil rights activist in South Africa (1893–1914)

  • 1993 -he took a job with an Indian firm in South Africa. There he became an effective advocate for Indian rights.
  • 1906 -In 1906 he first put into action satyagraha, his technique of nonviolent resistance. His success in South Africa gave him an international reputation.
  • 1915 -he returned to India and within a few years became the leader of a nationwide struggle for Indian home rule.

    • Struggle for Indian independence (1915–1947)

  • 1920 -Gandhi joined the Indian National Congress and was introduced to Indian issues, politics and the Indian people primarily by Gokhale.
  • 1930 -began escalating demands until on 26 January 1930 the Indian National Congress declared the independence of India.
  • 1942 -Gandhi and the Congress withdrew their support of the Raj when the Viceroy declared war on Germany in September 1939 without consultation. Tensions escalated until Gandhi demanded immediate independence in 1942 and the British responded by imprisoning him and tens of thousands of Congress leaders.
  • 1942–44 -Gandhi, who in 1934 had resigned as leader and member of the Indian National Congress, becomes politically active again early in World War II, demanding immediate independence as India’s price for aiding Britain in the war. He is imprisoned again, from 1942 to 1944.
  • 1947 -Finaly in 15 August 1947 India formally achieves independence from British rule. However, the partition of the subcontinent into India and Pakistan is a great disappointment to Gandhi, who has long worked for Hindu-Muslim unity. Rioting between Muslims and Hindus over the partition breaks out in many areas. Again Gandhi turns to nonviolence, fasting until Delhi rioters pledge peace.
  • 1948 -30 January 1948 it came with very bad day when Indian people got a news unfortunately he died in new Delhi.
